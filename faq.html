Flight attendant safety

Is this allowed in my union contract? What about Delta?

keyboard_arrow_down

We read the union contracts of American Airlines, United, Spirit, Jet Blue, Southwest Airlines, and Alaska. We found nothing indicating that flight attendants are prohibited from carrying items for someone else. There also was no language stating that you were prohibited from making additional income while doing so.

Since Delta has no union or public agreement, we recommend Delta flight attendants use the service only on personal travel as we learn more. For any questions reach out to letsgo@tugende.io.

How do you keep flight attendants safe?

keyboard_arrow_down

There are several steps we take to keep you safe.
1. Manual inspection. Every single page of a document is inspected front to back. We repeat this twice with two different inspectors.
2. Each document we receive has an audit trail of who inspected it and who approved it.
3. Any document that is gives us concern is either sent back or (in rare cases) handed off to the proper authorities.
4. We will run the document through language translation if needed.

This is just a taste of what we do to keep you safe; read our safety section to learn more. Your safety is our top priority.

Can I inspect the documents myself?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, you can! You will receive the document well before your flight so that you will have plenty of time to review the contents.

Can I still use KCM ?

keyboard_arrow_down

Unfortunately no. KCM  is strictly for your personal items. We  will send reminders before your trip.

Flight attendant compensation

When will I get paid?

keyboard_arrow_down

After you have mailed the per-addressed envelope in the destination country's postal system or have handed over the document to the hotel's front desk staff, reach out to us to provide confirmation and we will compensate you immediately.

How will I get paid?

keyboard_arrow_down

We can pay you through Venmo, PayPal, Cashapp, or check.
If none of those options work for you, just send us an email at letsgo@tugende.io and we can work something out.

We understand that things happen

What if I get reassigned after I receive the document?

keyboard_arrow_down

We work around you!
We understand you can be reassigned at any moment. Just let us know about the reassignment, and we will update with the sender.

Once you return home you can write "Return to Sender" on the envelope and mail the item back to us. However, if you have an upcoming flight to the document's destination you may keep the item and send the item over on your upcoming flight.

Can I change my mind after I receive the document?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes of course! All we ask is that you let us know.
On envelope that you receive simply write "Return to Sender" and drop off the document at any post office. We will never pressure you to do anything that you are not comfortable with.

Shipping Logistics

How do you keep my items safe? How do you verify travelers?

keyboard_arrow_down

We do 3 things to keep your items safe.
1. All travellers need to send us government issued identification.
2. We confirm and verify their flight.
3. Travellers will only get rewarded on every successful delivery.

When will my item get to the final destination?

keyboard_arrow_down

About 4 - 8 weeks but it may arrive even sooner. This is the time we need to find, screen, and verify travelers.

Will the traveler deliver the item to the final destination?

keyboard_arrow_down

No, once they land they will simply drop off your item using your country's delivery service or drop off your item at a previously agreed meeting point.

Document Safety

How do you verify if travellers completed their job?

keyboard_arrow_down

In order to earn their compensation, travellers need to prove that they brought your item to the airport, they landed with your item, and lastly they handed your item over to postal service or dropped it off at a previously agreed meeting point.

What happens if my item gets lost or stolen by the traveler?

keyboard_arrow_down

You will receive a full refund. We do everything in our power to ensure that this does not happen.

What happens if my item gets lost or stolen by my country's postal service?

keyboard_arrow_down

You will receive a partial refund. We will work with the postal service to find and locate your item. If you do not trust your country's postal system please let us know at support@tugnede.io and we can figure out another method of delivery.

Connecting your delivery with travelers

What if it takes longer than expected to find a traveler?

keyboard_arrow_down

We are confident that your item will be successfully delivered although it may take longer to find a courier heading to your target destination. If you change your mind before the item is in transit, you can ask for a full refund and receive your item back.

Will I get notified when a traveler is found?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes! And you will be informed of the date and time your item will lands in your destination. If for any reason the date is not correct, just let us know.

We adapt to your needs

Do you allow refunds?

keyboard_arrow_down

Absolutely, as long as your item is not already on its way to the final destination.
Let us know at support@tugende.io and we will process it right away.

My country's postal delivery service is unreliable. Can I still use this service?

keyboard_arrow_down

Yes, we can help you develop a final delivery strategy! Just reach out to us at support@tugende.io
