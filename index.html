Frequently Asked Questions

Got Questions? We got answers

Top Questions from Senders:

How do you keep my items safe?

keyboard_arrow_down

We do 3 things to keep your items safe.
1. All flight attendants need to present us with government issued identification and flight information.
2. We confirm and verify their flight.
3. Flight attendants will only get rewarded for every successful delivery.

How do you verify if flight attendants completed their job?

keyboard_arrow_down

In order to earn their compensation, flight attendants need to prove that they brought your item to the airport, they landed with your item, and lastly that they handed your item over to the postal service or dropped it off at a previously agreed meeting point.

Does the flight attendant deliver the item to the final destination?

keyboard_arrow_down

No, once they land they will simply drop off your item using your country's delivery service or drop off your item at a previously agreed meeting point.

Top Questions from Couriers:

How do you keep flight attendants safe?

keyboard_arrow_down

There are several steps we take to keep you safe.
1. Manual inspection. Every single page of a document is thoroughly inspected. We do this twice with two different inspectors.
2. Each document we receive has an audit trail of who inspected it and when.
3. Any document that gives us cause for concern is either sent back or (in rare cases) handed off to the proper authorities.
4. We will run the document through language translation if needed.

This is just a taste of what we do to keep you safe, read our safety section to learn more. Your safety is our top priority.

How will I get paid?

keyboard_arrow_down

We can pay you through Venmo, PayPal, Cash App, or check.
If none of those options work for you, just send us an email at letsgo@tugende.io and we can work something out.

What if I get reassigned after I receive the document?

keyboard_arrow_down

We work around you!
We understand you can be reassigned at any moment. Just let us know about the reassignment, and we will update with the sender.

Once you return home you can write "Return to Sender" on the envelope and mail the item back to us. However, if you have an upcoming flight to the document's destination you may keep the item and send the item over in your upcoming flight.
