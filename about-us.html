Our Story

Growing up in the US with Ugandan parents, it cost nearly $200 to just send a simple document back home. I knew there had to be a better way. Flights crisscross the globe constantly, and onboard each of them are flight attendants. We realized that we could help flight attendants earn more from their rotation while lowering shipping costs. We believe this is a win-win for everyone and it left us saying... "Tugende!" (Lets Go!)

- Richard Mpanga
