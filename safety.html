Our commitment to your safety

At Tugende, your safety is our top priority. We want you to earn additional income while maintaining peace of mind.  
To achieve this, we have implemented a comprehensive safety protocol inline with contemporary safety standards to detect any problematic items before they reach you.

A rigorous manual inspection

Every document goes through a meticulous 3-step inspection process.
1. Tactile examination for anomalies or irregularities
2. Visual inspection of the document and it’s contents
3. Physical shake test to identify any hidden discrepancies
We also detect abnormal orders and flag suspicious accounts.
‍

AI language translation

1. We read through each document to ensure that the contents are appropriate.
2. If the document is in a foreign language, we leverage AI language translation.
This allows us to understand its contents for further review.
3. If needed we will contact professional translators to parse the document contents.
‍
‍

Reject an item for any reason

We designed Tugende to allow flight attendants to reject an item at any time, even after receiving it. The process is simple:
1. Let us know: Email us about the concern
2. Reject It: Write "Rejected: Return to Sender" on the item.
3. Mail It: Mail the item before or after your trip back to us.
‍
With Tugende, you are in control.

Transparent audit trail

Every document has a transparent audit trail.
This trail records details about when the document was inspected and by whom, providing an additional layer of accountability and transparency.

Round-the-clock human support

We are here for you 24/7.
Our dedicated support team is ready to assist you at any hour of the day. We mean real-time support from humans, whenever you need it.

Background checks for employees

At Tugende, we ensure that all our employees undergo periodic background checks. This guarantees that our team is trustworthy and aligned with our mission to provide a secure platform.
